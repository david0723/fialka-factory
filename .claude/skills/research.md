# Research Skill

## Purpose

Gather and curate travel-related content that aligns with fialka's content pillars: myth-busting, ethical inquiry, niche discovery, personal narrative hooks, and cultural criticism.

## Primary Searches (run all)

1. "overtourism" OR "tourist overcrowding" + recent news
2. "ethical travel" OR "responsible tourism" + debate OR controversy
3. "off the beaten path" OR "hidden gem" + destination NOT listicle
4. "voluntourism" OR "volunteer abroad" + criticism OR debate
5. "digital nomad" OR "remote work abroad" + reality OR problems
6. "travel culture" OR "travel trend" + critique OR backlash
7. "budget travel" OR "backpacking" + dying OR changing

## Secondary Searches (if time permits)

1. "travel gentrification" + local impact
2. "passport privilege" OR "travel inequality"
3. "eco tourism" + greenwashing OR reality
4. "niche destination" + undiscovered OR underrated (for Niche Bucketlist)

## Sources to Watch

- Travel subreddits: r/solotravel, r/TravelNoPics, r/digitalnomad
- Substack travel writers (thoughtful ones, not influencers)
- Tourism industry reports (UNWTO, tourism boards)
- Ethical travel blogs and organizations
- Local news from popular tourist destinations
- Academic/longform: The Guardian travel, Lonely Planet Pathfinders, Atlas Obscura

## Topic Selection Criteria

- **Fialka's angle**: Can she bring personal experience or a contrarian take?
- **Ethical dimension**: Is there a moral question worth exploring?
- **Not obvious**: Would most travel blogs skip this topic?
- **Conversation starter**: Would this spark debate in comments?
- **Niche Bucketlist potential**: Is there an obscure destination worth profiling?

## Output Format

```markdown
# Research Notes - YYYY-MM-DD

## Myth-Busting
- **[Topic]**: [What's the myth? What's the reality?]. [Source URL]

## Ethical Inquiry
- **[Topic]**: [The ethical question]. [Different perspectives]. [Source URL]

## Niche Discovery
- **[Destination/Topic]**: [Why it's interesting]. [What makes it niche]. [Source URL]

## Cultural Criticism
- **[Trend]**: [What's happening]. [Why it matters]. [Source URL]

## Personal Narrative Hooks
- **[Topic]**: [How this connects to fialka's experiences]. [Potential angle]

## Interesting Threads
- [URL]: [Why worth reading for fialka]
```

## Quality Filters

- Would fialka actually care about this?
- Is this a genuine insight or just clickbait?
- Does this have a travel-ethics dimension?
- Skip: pure listicles, influencer drama, luxury travel promotion, generic "best beaches" content
